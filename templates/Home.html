This Chatbot serves as an innovative, interactive solution for homeowners looking to optimize energy consumption and reduce utility costs. Developed using Python and leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques with libraries like NLTK, the chatbot understands user queries and provides personalized energy-saving advice. Through dynamic and intuitive conversations, it gathers detailed information about household appliances, energy usage patterns, and lifestyle habits to generate actionable recommendations tailored to the user's specific needs. The chatbot's ability to deliver real-time advice during ongoing interactions makes it a step ahead of conventional, static energy management tools.
